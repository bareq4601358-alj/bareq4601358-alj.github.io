Privacy Policy — Vision Training VR

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Vision Training VR (the "App") is a virtual reality vision-training application. This policy explains how the App handles your information. The short version: the App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Information we collect

None. The App does not require an account, does not ask for any personal information, and does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. The App does not transmit any data about you to us or to any third party.

Local settings

Your preferences (such as target speed, size, color, routine, and eye mode) may be stored locally on your device so the App can remember them between sessions. This information stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

Permissions

The App runs as an immersive WebXR experience and uses only the headset tracking and controller input required to display and interact with the 3D scene. It does not request access to the microphone, camera images, location, contacts, or files.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
